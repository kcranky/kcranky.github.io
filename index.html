About

I'm an electrical and computer engineer living in Cape Town, South Africa. I work primarily with FPGAs and custom integration solutions for time sensitive networking. I am heavily interested in embedded systems and how technology can make day-to-day life simpler, so we can focus on the more important things.

I try to contribute to establishing systems and structures for prolonged learning, to make it easier for others to enter into the embedded systems space. A good showcase of this is the work I've done at UCT to assist in undergraduate learning. Visit the UCT open Courseware site to see my contributions.
